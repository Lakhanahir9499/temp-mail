Welcome to Temp Mail by Modverse

Temp Mail is your go-to tool for generating free, disposable email addresses that you can use anywhere on the internet without sharing your real identity. Whether you're signing up for a new account, testing services, or avoiding spam, our service gives you a fast, secure, and anonymous solution.

Benefits of Using Temporary Email

  • Instant access to disposable email addresses — no signup needed.
  • Protect your privacy by avoiding spam and unwanted promotional emails.
  • Safely test websites, services, and platforms before using your personal email.
  • Completely free, anonymous, and easy to use on any device.

How Temp Mail Works

Click the “Generate Email” button to receive a temporary address. Use it wherever needed. Then hit “Check Inbox” to see received messages in real-time. You can copy the email and reuse it until you refresh or generate a new one.

Who Should Use Temp Mail?

Anyone concerned about privacy or tired of spam. Whether you’re a developer, tester, shopper, or just a casual internet user, Temp Mail is a must-have tool to stay safe and anonymous online.
