Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

At ModVerse, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect:

We may collect limited information such as:

  • Telegram usernames or IDs (for bot interaction)
  • Temporary email addresses generated via our services
  • Device information (browser, IP address) for analytics and security

How We Use Your Information:

  • To provide you with services such as temporary mail and referral programs
  • To improve our platform based on anonymous analytics
  • To communicate updates or important changes

Data Security:

We use secure methods to store and handle data. However, no platform is completely immune to threats. We recommend not sharing sensitive information via our temporary mail services.

Third-Party Services:

We may use third-party APIs such as Mail.tm for temp mail. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage users to review them.

User Rights:

  • You can request deletion of your user data by contacting us via Telegram.
  • You are not required to provide personal information to use basic services.

Contact: For any concerns regarding privacy, please reach out via @lakhan_lakhnotra or our official Telegram channel.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
